About

The ancient city of Tirunelveli, once called "Nellai Seemai", remains a symbol of cultural richness and historical depth

Major Religions & Heritage

Nellaiyappar Kovil

Nellaiyappar Kovil is a magnificent ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in Tirunelveli. It is famous for its stunning Dravidian architecture and the musical pillars that produce melodic sounds.

Manappadu

Manappadu Church is a serene coastal shrine dedicated to St. Francis Xavier, located near Tiruchendur. It is known for its peaceful sea view and historical significance in South Indian Christian heritage.

Pallivasal

Aathanagari Pallivasal is a sacred mosque known for its spiritual atmosphere and beautiful architecture. It stands as a symbol of unity and faith, attracting devotees and visitors from various places.

Sacred & Scenic

Manimuthar-Falls

Manimuthar Falls is a breathtaking waterfall located near Tirunelveli, surrounded by lush green forests. It is a popular spot for nature lovers, offering a peaceful retreat with crystal-clear cascading waters.

Panpoli

Panpoli Murugan Kovil is a divine temple dedicated to Lord Murugan, nestled amidst scenic hills near Tirunelveli. It is known for its serene surroundings and attracts devotees seeking peace and spiritual blessings.

Sundarapandiyapuram

Sundarapandiyapuram Sunflower Field is a vibrant attraction near Tenkasi, famous for its golden blooms. It offers a picturesque view perfect for photography and nature lovers throughout the blooming season.

Discover the Beauty of Past and Nature

Mekkarai Dam

Mekkarai Dam is a scenic reservoir located near Sengottai in the Tirunelveli district of Tamil Nadu. It provides water for irrigation and serves as a peaceful picnic spot surrounded by lush greenery and hills.

Kattabomman Memorial Fort

The Kattabomman Memorial Fort is a historic museum dedicated to the brave freedom fighter Veerapandiya Kattabomman. Located in Tamil Nadu, the museum showcases his legacy, weapons, and stories of resistance against British rule.

Redaiyarpatti Hill

Redaiyarpatti Hill, located near Tirunelveli, is known for its serene environment and beautiful natural scenery. It is a popular spot for trekking and offers a panoramic view of the surrounding villages and landscapes.

Rituals & Performances

Manjolai

Manjolai estate is a scenic tea plantation in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu, located within the Kalakkad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve.

Coutrallam

Courtallam is a hill station and town in Tamil Nadu's Tenkasi District, famous for its therapeutic waterfalls on the Western Ghats, known as the "Spa of the South".
